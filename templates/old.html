STRUCTURE OVER PERSONALITY

Capital and Control Are Separated

Airvyb operates under a clear dual-entity model. This is not paperwork — it is protection.

MEMBER-OWNED

Airvyb Capital Pool (ACP)

Member-owned capital. Holds all contributed assets. Members have proportional economic rights.

Capital is strictly segregated.
INDEPENDENT MANAGEMENT

Airvyb Management Ltd (AML)

Independent management entity responsible for governance, reporting, deal sourcing, oversight, and operational infrastructure.

Member capital and operational funds never mix.

Transparency is structural — not optional.

The founder owns the management company. The founder does not own member capital. Incentives are aligned. Ownership boundaries are clear.
